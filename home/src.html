Teacher, Developer, Mathematical Illustrator

Hello! My name is Cruz, and I’m a graduate student in math at the University of Oregon. I’m particularly drawn to the beautiful visualizations that math can often create, and I’ve built a number of polished, high-performance web apps for exploring them. I’ve also created interactive notes for many of the classes I’ve taught in pursuit of quality teaching, and I’ve written a novel and a handful of short stories. All of that is collected here, and whether you’re looking for it or not, I think you’ll find something you enjoy.
