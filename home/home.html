Reading, Writing, and Proving

My name is Cruz Godar, and I’m a graduate student in the mathematics department at the University of Oregon. I’m particularly drawn to the beautiful visualizations some areas of math can create, and I spend some of my free time building polished, high-performance web apps for exploring them. I’ve also written a novel and a handful of short stories, and along with the materials for the classes I teach, all of that’s hosted on this site. Whether you’re here for Caligo, complex maps, or calculus notes, you’re in the right place.
