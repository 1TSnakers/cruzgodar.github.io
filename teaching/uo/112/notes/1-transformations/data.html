Trigonometry is Latin for the study of triangles, but it goes quite a bit further. During our course, we’ll learn how to transform functions to vastly extend their possible applications, introduce functions to measure points on the unit circle, and use vectors to represent quantities with magnitudes and directions. This is one of the most broadly-useful math courses there is, and its tools and techniques extend to dozens of other math courses.
