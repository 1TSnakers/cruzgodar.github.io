First of all, we'll need a few definitions of vector field concepts ### def sources, sinks, and saddles In a vector field, a **critical point** is a point $(x, y)$ for which there is no motion. If the field around a critical point exclusively flows away from it, the point is called a **source**. If the surrounding field exclusively flows toward the point, it's called a **sink**. Otherwise, the point is called a **saddle point**. ### In the field below, the top-right critical point is a source, the bottom-left is a sink, and the other two are saddle points. ### canvas vector-field ### nav-buttons
