!header Kleinian Pearls !begin-text-block Quasi-Fuchsian groups are groups generated by two transformations of the complex plane, and their limit sets form intricate fractals. We can apply the same transformations in 3-dimensional space to create a similar fractal — the math involved limits us to a specific type of group, equivalent to moving only the first dot in the 2D applet — but the structures are remarkable nevertheless. Drag on the scene to look around. On a keyboard, use WASD to move, and on a touchscreen, hold with two fingers to move forward and three to move back.
