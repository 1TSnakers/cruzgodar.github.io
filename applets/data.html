Applets

Some of the coding projects that I’ve made and thought others might enjoy. Everything here is made with Wilson, a library I wrote to make high-quality applets easier to create. Have a look at the code if you’re interested in how these work.

Stills and animations from these applets have been featured in the 2022 and 2023 Bridges conferences, the Jordan Schnitzer Museum of Art, the Erb Memorial Union, the 2020 UO Research as Art Competition, the Girls’ Angle Bulletin magazine, and UO’s University Hall.
